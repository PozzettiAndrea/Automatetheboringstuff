^ = at the beginning
? = at the end
^(.*)? = at the beginning and the end
.sth = ends with sth
.sth{1,2} = at least 1 at most 2 more chars before
.* = anythinggggggg. But not newlines.
.*, re.DOTALL = truly anything including newlines
something (example: [aeiou]), re.IGNORECASE = case insensitive
re.I = re.IGNORECASE
